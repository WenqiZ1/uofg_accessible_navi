Welcome to the Accessible Routing Map

This WebGIS provides accessible pedestrian routing across the University of Glasgow’s Gilmorehill campus. Choose from different routing modes and map styles:

    Available Route Modes

  • 🚶 All Access Route: General pedestrian routing, may include stairs and steeper segments if they provide the shortest-time path.
  • ♿ Step-Free Route: Avoiding stairs and penalising steep slopes and uneven surfaces.

    • Map Styles

  • 🗺️ Detailed Map: Standard cartography with contextual detail.
  • 👁️ High Contrast Map: Clear, bold symbology to aid legibility for low-vision users.
  • 🛰️ Satellite Imagery: Real-world imagery for environmental context and orientation.

    • Data sources:

    50 cm DTM (Scottish Government), OpenStreetMap (street network).
